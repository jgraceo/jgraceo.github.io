My name is Grace! I am a student at Northeastern. I love to read and (sometimes) write. This portfolio was made in a digital writing class. It is my first time using HTML, although I have worked with XML and TEI in the past. I am still learning how to add images, make pages, and implement larger format changes. I am hoping that as I have more free time I can transition this site into a space where I can share my research and work I am proud of.
