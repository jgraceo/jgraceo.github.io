welcome to my wacky, creaky, and maybe even clunky website

This is an online portfolio that I created for a digital writing class. I have taken liberties with grammar as well as the more traditional understanding of professionalism. I am still learning how HTML works and there are bound to be errors and mistakes within this space. This website contains a collection of some of my work. Class assignments, past writing, and personal research can be found within the pages of this portfolio.

This site is still being updated. It is under construction. You will have to be a little patient during your navigation.
