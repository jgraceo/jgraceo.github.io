welcome to my wacky, creaky, and maybe even gawky website!

This is an online portfolio I created for a digital writing class that is slowly evolving into a personal website. I have taken liberties with grammar and ignored traditional understandings of professionalism. I want this space to be unrestricted by expectation and represent my personal and academic interests. I am still learning HTML, and there are bound to be errors and mistakes within these pages. This website contains a collection of some of my work and current exploration. Class assignments, published articles, and personal research are some of the things that can be found here.

This site is constantly being updated and changed. Portions are actively under construction. You will have to be a little patient during your navigation. I have made sure to specify when a page is still being developed.
